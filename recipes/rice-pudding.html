What's better than Rice Pudding!


Let me tell you about the ultimate cozy hug in a bowl—creamy, dreamy rice pudding.
It’s like your grandma’s secret recipe but with a dash of magic. Perfect for rainy days, heartaches, or just because you deserve something sweet.
Let’s do this!

What you'll need!



  • 1 cup of rice (short-grain or Arborio works best)
  • 4 cups of milk (whole milk for creaminess, but you can use almond or coconut milk for a twist)
  • 1/2 cup of sugar (or to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon (optional, but why not?)
  • Raisins or nuts for topping (totally optional, but they add a nice crunch)

Let’s Get Cooking

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. This helps remove excess starch.
  2. In a large pot, combine the rice, milk, sugar, salt, and cinnamon. Bring it to a gentle boil over medium heat.
  3. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer. Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
  4. Cook for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is tender and the mixture is creamy. If it gets too thick, add a splash more milk.
  5. Stir in the vanilla extract and let it cool slightly before serving.
  6. Top with raisins or nuts if you like, and sprinkle a little extra cinnamon on top for good measure.
  7. Serve warm or chilled, and enjoy the cozy vibes!
