What you will need (Ingredienta)



  • Red Sauce
    • 500g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork for extra flavor)
    • 1 large onion, finely chopped
    • 3 garlic cloves, minced
    • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 can (400g) tomato sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • teaspoon dried basil (or fresh if you have it!)
    • 1 teaspoon sugar (balances the acidity)
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • Olive oil for cooking
  • Bachamel Sauce
    • 50g butter (about 3.5 tbsp)
    • 50g all-purpose flour (about ⅓ cup)
    • 500ml milk (2 cups, warm)
    • Salt and black pepper, to taste
    • A pinch of nutmeg (trust me, this makes all the difference!)
    • 50g grated Parmesan (optional but amazing)
  • 12 lasagna sheets (no-boil or regular)
  • 250g mozzarella cheese, shredded

Magic time! (Steps)

  1. Cook the Meat Sauce
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Sauté the onions until soft and golden, then add the garlic.
    3. Add the ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
    4. Stir in the tomato paste, cook for a minute, then add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
    5. Let it simmer for 30 minutes to deepen the flavors.
  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce
    1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in the flour and cook for 1-2 minutes to get rid of the raw flour taste.
    3. Gradually add warm milk, whisking constantly to keep it smooth.
    4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens (about 3-5 minutes).
    5. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg. Stir in Parmesan if using.
  3. Assemble the Lasagna
    Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). In a baking dish:
    1. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom.
    2. >Add a layer of lasagna sheets.
    3. Spoon over some béchamel.
    4. Sprinkle some mozzarella.
    5. Add another layer of meat sauce.
    6. Repeat the layers until you run out, finishing with a final layer of béchamel and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella + Parmesan.
  4. Bake to Perfection
    1. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    3. Let it rest for 10 minutes before cutting (I know it’s hard, but it helps everything set).
