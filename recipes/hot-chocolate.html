Hot Chocolate


This isn’t just a drink—it’s comfort in a cup, like a cozy hug on a chilly day.
And forget the instant stuff, because homemade hot chocolate? It’s rich, creamy, and absolutely dreamy.


  • ingredients

    • 2 cups of milk (whole milk for creaminess, or any milk you prefer)
    • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
    • 2 tablespoons of sugar (or to taste)
    • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla extract
    • A pinch of salt
    • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping (optional, but highly recommended)

  • How to make it:

    1. In a small saucepan, combine the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt.
    2. Add a splash of milk and whisk until smooth.
    3. Gradually add the rest of the milk while whisking to combine.
    4. Heat over medium heat until hot but not boiling, stirring occasionally.
    5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
    6. Pour into your favorite mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows if you’re feeling extra indulgent.
    7. Take a moment to appreciate your creation, then dive in!
